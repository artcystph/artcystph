Our Story

artcyst.ph

"Handcrafted for minds in motion."

Artcyst.ph began in 2018 as a small craft studio founded by me and my sister, creating paintings and calligraphy rooted in a shared love for handmade work. The name Artcyst draws from “art” and “cyst” (spoken like “sis”) — a quiet nod to our bond, and to catalyst: something that sparks ideas into motion.

After a pause in 2020, Artcyst returns in 2026 with a more focused expression. Today, the studio centers on handcrafted leather journals and folios, while my sister continues her own creative path in her bouquet business. Though our mediums have changed, the heart of what we started remains the same and so does the name.

Every folio we make is assembled by hand, one piece at a time — each cord chosen with purpose, each leather panel pressed and trimmed with care.

We remain a small studio based in the Ilocos Norte, Philippines. Every order sustains the craft, and for that, we are deeply grateful.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your folio.

How long does production take?+
We hand-assemble every folio to order. Production takes 7–10 business days before we hand it off to the courier. You'll receive a shipping notification once it's on its way.
What leather do you use?+
All our folios are made with premium vegan leather — smooth, durable, and cruelty-free. Colors are photographed as accurately as possible, but slight variation may occur between screens.
How do I care for the leather?+
To keep your folio looking its best:

• Wipe clean with a soft, slightly damp cloth. Avoid soaking.
• Keep away from direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent fading.
• Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.
• Do not use alcohol-based cleaners or harsh solvents — these can damage the vegan leather surface.
• A thin layer of leather conditioner can be applied sparingly every few months.
Can I change or cancel my order?+
Orders can be modified within 24 hours of placement. After that, production may have already begun. Please reach out to us directly on our social media channels: @artcyst.ph as soon as possible if you need to make changes.
Do you ship overseas?+
Currently, we only ship within the Philippines (Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao). International shipping is something we're working toward — Follow @artcyst.ph across our social media platforms to keep up with new releases and announcements!
How does engraving work?+
The engraving is manually etched into the leather, creating a lasting impression. Up to 5 characters are included free; 6–10 characters incur a ₱50 surcharge. Choose your font style and placement zone during customization.
Custom Requests — what if I want something not listed?+
We'd love to hear your ideas! For custom orders, special color requests, or anything not available in our current options, contact us directly via our social media platforms: @artcyst.ph. We'll do our best to accommodate you — note that custom requests may have longer lead times and adjusted pricing.
